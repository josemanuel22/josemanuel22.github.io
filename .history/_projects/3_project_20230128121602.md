[Repository](https://github.com/josemanuel22/LatexBVSapacePackage) Latex package that extends existing packages over functional spaces. It includes $$L^{p}$$ spaces, Sobolev spaces, lips spaces, $$BV$$ and $$SBV$$ spaces, all with a facilitated syntax and in addition to other useful functions.
